About the Occult Research Team

Welcome to the Occult World Cup and Encyclopedia—the ultimate digital library dedicated to the world's most enduring mysteries, cryptids, and paranormal anomalies.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide a comprehensive, well-researched, and engaging platform for exploring the unknown. From the dense pine forests housing the Jersey Devil to the remote mountain passes of Dyatlov, we compile historical accounts, eyewitness testimonies, and scientific theories.

Who We Are

The Occult Research Team consists of passionate folklorists, cryptid enthusiasts, and independent researchers. We spend countless hours verifying sources, comparing legends across cultures, and presenting the highest quality information to our readers. Our interactive "World Cup" tournament format allows the community to participate in deciding which mystery holds the greatest cultural impact.

Editorial Policy

We believe in maintaining an objective, critical, yet open-minded approach. All our encyclopedia entries are carefully curated to ensure they reflect both the popular mythology and the underlying factual history of each phenomenon.
